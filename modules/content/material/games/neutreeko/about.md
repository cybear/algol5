{GAME} was invented by Jan Kristian Haugland in 2001. It is based of the {GAME:id=neutron} and Teeko games.

{PIC:name=physicalneutreeko.jpg,cred=Image by Niecodziennik,credurl=https://rygalo.com/2013/02/04/neutreeko/,title=Physical Neutreeko board}

This heritage makes for a highly tactical game, with both players trying to outsmart each other. {GAME} is fast phased, with little room for errors. Don't get stuck in the corners!
